About Us

Welcome!

Hello, welcome to Rehab Right, an online based rehabilitation company dedicated to helping you recover from injuries, aches, and pains, so you can continue to do the things you love.
Rehab right was founded from the idea that many people who suffer injuries are busy individuals that want an easy to follow, step-by-step, simple, scientifically backed plan to help them recover. Life’s busy, and sometimes you don’t have time to get to a doctor or a physical therapist. This is where rehab right comes in.

The idea for Rehab Right came from personal experience with injuries along with from helping family and friends work through injuries, aches, and pain to return to the activities they love.

Where Do the Programs come from?


All of the programs have been created by a licensed healthcare professional and are designed using science as the foundation.

All of our programs are backed by science. They start with an overview to help try to educate you about the injury along with some simple anatomy. We feel that by gaining some simple education, it can help you understand why you are doing what you are doing which will ultimately help you aid recovery. From there, a step by step program can be followed to help you get back to the activities you love and work to help you remain injury free.
